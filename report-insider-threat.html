Case #2024-001: Insider Threat

Date of Investigation: March 15, 2024

Status: Closed

1. Executive Summary

On March 10, 2024, our firm was contracted to investigate a suspected intellectual property theft incident. The investigation confirmed that a departing senior developer successfully exfiltrated 4GB of proprietary source code by transferring the data to a personal Google Drive account prior to their termination date.

2. Evidence Acquired

  • EVIDENCE-01: Forensic Image (E01) of subject's workstation (Asset ID: WK-492)
  • EVIDENCE-02: Network flow logs from the corporate firewall covering March 1 - March 15
  • EVIDENCE-03: Office 365 Audit Logs

3. Timeline of Events

Timestamp (UTC) Action Source Artifact
2024-03-05 09:14:22 Subject logged into personal Google Drive via Chrome Chrome History (History.sqlite)
2024-03-05 09:45:10 Created ZIP archive of `C:\Project_Omega\` (size: 4.1GB) NTFS $MFT, Windows Prefetch
2024-03-05 09:50:00 Upload initiated to Google Drive Firewall NetFlow Logs

4. Recommendations

To prevent future occurrences, we strongly advise implementing a Data Loss Prevention (DLP) solution to block unauthorized uploads to personal cloud storage domains. Additionally, USB mass storage should be disabled via Group Policy for all non-essential personnel.
